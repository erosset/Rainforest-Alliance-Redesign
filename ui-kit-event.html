The Rainforest Alliance will hold its most prestigious event, the annual gala dinner and award show, on Wednesday, May 11, 2016.

Conservationists, innovators and renowned leaders in the business and diplomatic communities will gather at the American Museum of Natural History to recognize the outstanding achievements made by individuals and companies in the areas of sustainable agriculture, forestry and tourism.

The evening's program includes a brief presentation of the Sustainable Standard-Setter and Lifetime Achievement awards, live entertainment, a silent auction and a formal dinner.

Proceeds from the gala benefit the comprehensive conservation initiatives of the Rainforest Alliance that enable us to ensure sustainable livelihoods and conserve biodiversity.
