Smallholder Palm Oil Financing in Indonesia: Needs and Opportunities for Sustainable Finance Intervention

Over the summer of 2015, the Rainforest Alliance researched palm oil financing in Indonesia with the objective to better understand the financing needs of smallholder palm oil producers and to identify opportunities for supporting these producers in accessing short term and long term financing.
