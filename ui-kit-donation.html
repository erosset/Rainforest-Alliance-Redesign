Ways to Give

Monthly Giving

You will make an important commitment to conservation while giving us the stability to expand our efforts and plan for the future.

One-Time Gift

By donating to the Rainforest Alliance, you'll become a member of a worldwide conservation movement that grows each day.

Canopy Associates

Contribute $1,000 or more and join a group whose support has a significant impact on ecosystems and communities around the world.
Other Ways to Give

Honor Gifts

Whether it’s a tribute to the memory of a loved one or in celebration of a special occasion, your gift to the Rainforest Alliance is a thoughtful way to honor others and protect the planet.

Personal Fundraising

Become a sustainable hero and turn parties, walk-a-thons and other special moments into conservation drives. Build your own personal fundraising page, set a goal, and invite friends and family to donate to a great cause.

Stock

Making a stock donation is an efficient and impactful way to support the Rainforest Alliance. You’ll be investing in the success of sustainable communities that are making global markets greener.

Estate Planning

A planned gift is a wonderful way to ensure the longevity of the Rainforest Alliance’s work. Help safeguard the earth’s treasures for future generations while arranging your own environmental legacy.

Corporate Donations

The Rainforest Alliance gives corporations the chance to become more sustainable. Now, your company can give back through grants, sponsorship or cause marketing, workplace giving and more.

Events

From celebrity-studded galas honoring champions of conservation to adventurous Kenyan eco-trips, the Rainforest Alliance holds a number of events that help you support our work while making indelible memories.

Robert W. Wilson Innovation Fund

This fund enables the Rainforest Alliance to pursue new opportunities in sustainability that are inextricably linked to the preservation of biodiversity and improvement of farmer and forester livelihoods.

Mail Your Gift

If you prefer to mail your gift, please send our printable form to: Rainforest Alliance; 233 Broadway; 28th Floor New York, NY 10279. For questions or comments, please send an email to development@ra.org or call us directly, (888) MY-EARTH or 1-212-677-1900.
